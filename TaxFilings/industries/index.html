Specialized Industry Tax & Accounting Solutions

Every industry faces unique tax challenges and CRA guidelines. We provide specialized compliance support for every major sector.

Healthcare

Specialized accounting for medical doctors, dentists, chiropractors, and clinics.

Real Estate

Tax strategies and accounting for real estate agents, brokers, and property owners.

Construction

Progress billing, job costing, subcontractor T5018 filing, and tax advice for builders.

E-Commerce

Multi-channel sales tax tracking, inventory accounting, Shopify/Amazon integrations.

Restaurants

Tips tracking, cost of goods sold (COGS) analysis, inventory control, and payroll.

Professional Services

Bookkeeping, corporate tax, and advisory for consultants, designers, and lawyers.

Technology

SR&ED tax credits support, cash burn tracking, and Virtual CFO services for tech startups.

Manufacturing

Cost accounting, inventory valuation, work-in-progress (WIP) tracking, and corporate tax.

Transportation

Logistics accounting, fuel tax tracking, driver payroll, and corporate tax returns.

Non-Profit
